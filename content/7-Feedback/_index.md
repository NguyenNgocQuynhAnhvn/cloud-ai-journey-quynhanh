### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
During my internship at FCAJ, I had the opportunity to work in a professional, dynamic, and collaborative environment. Team members were always approachable, open to discussion, and willing to provide support whenever I encountered challenges in my work. This allowed me to quickly adapt to the workplace and become more confident when taking on assigned tasks. In addition, the workflow was well organized, making it easier to track progress and complete tasks according to schedule.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
My mentor provided continuous guidance and detailed explanations of both technical topics and work processes whenever I faced difficulties. Rather than simply providing answers, my mentor encouraged me to explore solutions independently and develop my own problem-solving skills. This approach significantly improved my analytical thinking and ability to handle tasks effectively. Furthermore, the management team and colleagues offered sufficient documentation, clear procedures, and continuous support, enabling me to complete my assignments efficiently.

**3. Value Gained from the Internship Program**  
The internship program at FCAJ helped bridge the gap between academic knowledge and real-world industry requirements. Through the assigned tasks, I was able to apply the knowledge I had learned at university while becoming familiar with project development processes, team collaboration, and professional working standards. These practical experiences provided valuable insights that are difficult to gain solely through classroom learning.

**4. Skills Developed**  
The internship provided me with numerous opportunities to enhance both my technical expertise and soft skills. I was introduced to new technologies while improving my teamwork, communication, time management, and problem-solving abilities in a professional environment. In addition, the guidance and experience shared by my mentor and colleagues helped me gain a clearer understanding of my career direction and the practical expectations of the industry.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
I highly appreciate FCAJ's working culture, which emphasizes professionalism, mutual respect, and collaboration among team members. Everyone was willing to share their knowledge and support one another in achieving common goals. This positive atmosphere helped me integrate into the workplace quickly and gave me greater confidence throughout my internship.

### Suggestions & Expectations

**1. Suggestions for Improvement:**
To further enhance the internship program, I would recommend organizing more knowledge-sharing sessions, technology seminars, and career development workshops. These activities would provide interns with a broader understanding of the company while creating additional opportunities to learn from professionals across different fields.

**2. Future Expectations:**
Given the opportunity, I would be delighted to continue working with FCAJ through future training programs or projects to further strengthen my professional knowledge and gain additional practical experience. I would also be happy to recommend FCAJ's internship program to my friends and fellow students, as it provides an excellent environment for learning, developing professional skills, and preparing for future careers.
